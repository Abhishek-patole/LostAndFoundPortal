About This Portal

The Lost & Found Portal is a community-driven platform to help people report lost items, post found items, and coordinate recovery through events and communication. It’s designed to be simple, transparent, and accessible on desktop or mobile.

Our Mission

To reduce the stress of losing personal belongings and increase the chance of reunification by providing a centralized portal for reporting and events.

How It Works

  1. Register as a user.
  2. Report lost or found items with details.
  3. Check upcoming recovery events.
  4. Connect with owners or finders via contact info.
